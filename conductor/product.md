# Initial Concept
The project is a "Foreign Policy & Foreign Affairs Summaries" application. It consists of a Flask backend that serves article summaries from a SQLite database and a Flutter frontend for displaying them.

# Product Definition

## Target Audience
- Policy professionals and researchers.
- Students of international relations.
- General public interested in global affairs.

## Core Goals
- Help users stay informed on global issues with minimal time commitment.
- Provide a centralized archive of key foreign policy analysis (future goal).

## Key Features
- **Daily Automated Summaries:** Automatic extraction and summarization of new articles from Foreign Policy and Foreign Affairs.
- **Mobile-First Flutter App:** A clean, distraction-free interface for reading summaries on the go.
- **Article Database:** A persistent store for processed summaries (SQLite).

## User Experience (UX)
- **Mobile-First:** Optimized for mobile devices.
- **Distraction-Free:** Minimalist UI focused on reading content.

## Content Tone & Style
- **Objective and Professional:** Summaries should maintain a professional and unbiased tone.
- *Note: Existing summarization prompts are to be preserved.*
